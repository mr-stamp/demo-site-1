Ma's Famous Brownies

  1. Prep Work

    • Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
    • Rest the butter to room temperature
    • Wash your grubbers

  2. Mix Dry Ingredients

    1. In a large mixing bowl, add 2 cups of all-purpose flour, 1 cup of brown sugar, and 1 cup of cocoa powder. Stir ingredients thoroughly.
    2. Add in 1 tablespoon of baking powder. Also 1 tsp of baking soda.
    3. Set aside.

  3. Mix Wet Ingredients

    1. In a medium mixing bowl, break two large eggs. Whisk until consistent.
    2. Add 1 tablespoon of vanilla extract and a half teaspoon of salt to the egg mixture.
    3. Pour a half cup of milk into the wet mix.

  4. Combine Mixtures

    1. Place the large mixing bowl with the dry ingredients in a stand mixer on low setting.
    2. Slowly pour in the wet ingredients until incorporated.
    3. Drop 8 tablespoons of room temperature butter into the mixer. Consistency should be fluid but slow.

  5. Pour and Bake

    1. Grease an 8 by 11 inch pan.
    2. Pour mix into the pan. Use a spatula to smooth it out.
    3. Place in oven and bake for 20 minutes or until toothpick is clean.

  6. Bon Apetit
